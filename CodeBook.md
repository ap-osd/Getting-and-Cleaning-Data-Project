'Acc' accelerometer;  'Gyro' gyroscope 3-axial raw signals
'Mean' to indicate mean; 'Std' to indicate standard deviation
'Jerk' signals
'X','Y','Z' is used to denote 3-axial signals in the X, Y and Z directions
